---
title: "Managing Your Team"
description: "How to invite technicians, manage roles, and remove users"
---

The Team page in the dashboard shows all members of your company account and lets admins manage access.

## Roles

| Role | Access |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full dashboard access, can invite/remove members, can configure webhooks |
| **Tech** | Android app only, records attestations, no dashboard access |

## Inviting a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Team page">
    In the dashboard, click **Team** in the left navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send an invite">
    Click **Invite member**, enter their email address, and select their role. Click **Send invite**.

    They will receive an email with a link to set their password and download the Forager app if they are a tech.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm they appear in the list">
    Invited members appear immediately in the team list with a **Pending** badge until they accept the invite and log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing a member

Click the menu next to a team member's name and select **Remove**. Their account is deactivated immediately. Any attestations they previously recorded remain in the system.

<Warning>
  Removing a member cannot be undone from the dashboard. Contact Forager support if you need to reinstate a removed user.
</Warning>

## Last active

The **Last Active** column shows when each member last used the Forager app or dashboard. Use this to identify inactive accounts that should be removed.
