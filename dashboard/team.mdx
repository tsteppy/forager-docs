The Team page in the dashboard is the central place for admins to manage who has access to your Forager company account.

## Roles

| Role | Access |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full dashboard access — can invite members, change roles, remove users, configure webhooks, import/export assets, and manage site assignments |
| **User** | Android app only — records attestations and runs surveys. Sees only the buildings they have been assigned to. No dashboard access. |

## Inviting a team member

Only admins can send invites. The invite is sent by email and the new member downloads the Forager Android app directly from the link.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Team page">
    Log into the dashboard at [dashboard.hiveriselabs.com](https://dashboard.hiveriselabs.com) and click **Team** in the left navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the technician's email">
    In the **Invite Team Member** card, enter their email address and click **Send Invite**. The invite is sent immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Technician accepts the invite">
    The technician receives an email with a secure link. When they click it:
    1. Their account is created and verified automatically — no password required at this step.
    2. They land on a welcome page with a **Get it on Google Play** button.
    3. They download the Forager app and sign in with the email address they received the invite on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Technician appears in your team list">
    Once they have signed in, they appear in the Team member list. Their role is set to **User** by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign the technician to their sites">
    After sending an invite, a prompt appears on the Team page reminding you to assign the new member to their buildings. Click **Go to Sites →** to open the Sites page and add them.

    Until you assign them to at least one site, they will see an empty screen in the app and cannot confirm any assets.

    <Card title="Managing Sites" icon="building" href="/dashboard/sites">
      How to assign techs to buildings and control site-level access.
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  New technicians do not need to set a password to start using the app. If they ever need one (e.g., to log in on a new device after the invite link expires), they can use the **Forgot Password** flow on the login screen.
</Note>

## Changing a member's role

Click the role badge on any team member's row to toggle between **Admin** and **User**. The change takes effect immediately.

You cannot change your own role. If you need to demote yourself, ask another admin to do it.

## Removing a member

Click the trash icon on a member's row. A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm to remove them — they lose access to the company account immediately.

Any attestations they previously recorded remain in the system and are not affected.

<Warning>
  Removing a member cannot be undone from the dashboard. Contact support@hiveriselabs.com if you need to reinstate a removed user.
</Warning>

## Sites column

The **Sites** column shows which buildings each technician is assigned to. Admins show **All Sites**. Techs show the names of their assigned buildings, or **—** if they have no assignments yet.

Click **Sites** in the left navigation to manage assignments.

## Last active

The **Last Active** column shows when each member last recorded an attestation in the Forager app. Use this to identify inactive accounts.
