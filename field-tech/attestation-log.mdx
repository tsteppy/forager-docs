---
title: "Attestation Log"
description: "How to review your confirmation history in the Forager app"
---

The Attestation Log shows every confirmation your team has recorded — matches, mismatches, and new assets discovered. Use it to verify that your scans were recorded, review what happened during a session, or look up when a specific device was last confirmed.

## Opening the Attestation Log

From the Forager home screen, tap **Attestation Log**.

## Reading the log

Each entry shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Asset tag** | The barcode that was scanned |
| **Type** | Match, mismatch confirmed, mismatch dismissed, or new asset |
| **Location** | The room recorded at the time of the confirmation |
| **Tech** | Who performed the scan |
| **Time** | When the confirmation was recorded |

Entries are sorted with the most recent at the top.

## Searching

Type in the search bar to find entries by asset tag or location. Results update as you type. Tap **✕** to clear the search and return to the full list.

## Filtering

Tap a filter chip at the top of the screen to narrow the list by result type:

- **Match** — confirmed in expected location
- **Mismatch** — asset found in unexpected location
- **New asset** — first time this asset tag was seen by Forager

The type filter and search work together — for example, filter to **Mismatch** and search for a room name to find all mismatches in a specific area.

## What the log is used for

**Day-to-day:** If a scan did not seem to register, check the log to confirm the record was written. Look for the asset tag and the timestamp matching when you scanned.

**Compliance reviews:** Your IT director or manager can pull a full attestation history from the web dashboard **Reports** page, including CSV export. The in-app log is a quick reference for individual techs.

**Mismatch follow-up:** If you dismissed a mismatch earlier in the session, you can find the record here to confirm the asset tag and original location in case you need to follow up.

<Note>
  The in-app log shows recent records. For full historical reporting, filtered by date range, location, or tech, use the **Reports** page at [dashboard.hiveriselabs.com](https://dashboard.hiveriselabs.com).
</Note>
