The home screen is your starting point for all field work. Every function — scanning, surveying, reviewing records, and navigating to assets — is one tap away from here.

## Header

**Forager Dashboard** — The app title, always visible at the top.

### Status icons

The row of icons in the top-right corner shows the live state of your device's radios and data connection. From left to right:

| Icon | Name | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| Gold pin | Location | GPS is active. Solid gold means a location fix is confirmed. |
| Bluetooth symbol | Bluetooth | The BLE radio is on and scanning for nearby signals. |
| Circular target | RF Scanner | The app is actively reading Wi-Fi and Bluetooth signals to determine your room. |
| Up/down arrows | Sync | Data is syncing to the Forager server. Animated when a sync is in progress. |
| Cloud | Upload | Attestation records are queued or uploading. Clears once all records are sent. |
| Initials circle | Your profile | Tap to view your account, switch roles, or sign out. |

All icons should be active before you begin scanning assets. If Bluetooth or Location appears inactive, check that both are enabled in your phone's Settings.

## Site picker

If your admin has assigned you to more than one building, a **site chip** appears below the header. It shows the currently active site — tap it to open a dropdown and switch to a different building or select **All Sites** to see everything across all your locations at once.

When a site is selected the chip appears highlighted. Your selection is saved and restored the next time you open the app.

If you have only one site assignment, the site picker does not appear — your view is automatically scoped to that building.

<Note>
  If you open the app and see **"No sites assigned"**, your admin has not yet assigned you to any buildings. Contact your Forager admin to request access. You cannot confirm assets until at least one site is assigned to you.
</Note>

---

## Location indicator

A pill below the site picker shows your current detected location. It updates as the app reads RF signals from your surroundings:

| State | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Room name · Floor N** | Room locked in — ready to scan |
| **Floor N · locating room…** | Floor identified, still narrowing down the room |
| *(not visible)* | No floor or room detected yet — check radios |

Wait until a specific room name appears before scanning assets. Location accuracy improves once the room is confirmed.

<Note>
  If the indicator stays on "locating room…" for more than 30 seconds, check that both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth are enabled. The app needs both radios to determine your room.
</Note>

## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions grid gives you one-tap access to every field function.

### Survey Mode

Captures RF signal fingerprints for a room. Used during initial setup and any time a room's physical environment changes significantly — new walls, major furniture rearrangement, or access point changes.

Select your floor and room, then tap **Capture**. Hold still for about 30 seconds while the app records signals. Repeat for each room on the floor.

<Card title="Survey Mode" icon="map" href="/field-tech/survey-mode">
  Full guide: running a survey, how many captures to take, and when to re-survey.
</Card>

### Coverage

Shows a color-coded view of how thoroughly each room has been surveyed:

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Green** | Well surveyed — ready for attestation |
| **Yellow** | Thin coverage — consider re-surveying |
| **Gray** | Not yet surveyed |

Check Coverage after a survey session to confirm all rooms are green before starting attestation work.

<Card title="Coverage" icon="chart-bar" href="/field-tech/coverage">
  Full guide: reading the coverage map and improving thin rooms.
</Card>

### Presence Confirmation

The main daily workflow. Opens active scanning mode where the app detects your current room and records an attestation each time you scan an asset barcode.

The app compares the asset's recorded location to the room you are standing in and marks it as confirmed or flagged. This is where field techs spend most of their time.

<Card title="Scanning Assets" icon="barcode" href="/field-tech/scanning-assets">
  Full guide: the attestation workflow, handling mismatches, and tips for accurate scans.
</Card>

### Attestation Log

A history of every asset scan you have recorded. Each entry shows the asset tag, room, timestamp, and whether it was a match or a mismatch.

Use this to review your work, verify a specific scan was recorded, or investigate a flagged discrepancy before reporting it.

<Card title="Attestation Log" icon="list" href="/field-tech/attestation-log">
  Full guide: filtering the log, understanding match and mismatch records.
</Card>

### Signal Diagnostic

A real-time view of the raw Wi-Fi and Bluetooth signals the app is detecting. Useful for troubleshooting room detection issues or verifying the radio environment is healthy before a survey.

Can export a signal snapshot as a file — used when contacting support about a location accuracy problem.

<Card title="Signal Diagnostic" icon="signal" href="/field-tech/signal-diagnostic">
  Full guide: reading the diagnostic view and exporting a snapshot for support.
</Card>

### Find Device

Navigates you to an asset's last confirmed location. Enter or scan an asset tag and the app shows the floor plan with the asset's last known room highlighted. The indicator updates as you move through the building.

Use this when a device cannot be found in its expected location.

<Card title="Device Hunt" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/field-tech/device-hunt">
  Full guide: starting a device hunt, reading the floor plan, and what to do if the asset is not there.
</Card>

### Most Wanted

A prioritized queue of assets that need confirmation, ranked by how long they have been unseen. Tap any card to go directly to scanning mode for that asset. The queue also has a **Leaderboard** tab showing how your team ranks by confirmation points.

When an admin has an active sprint running, the Most Wanted card turns orange as a visual cue that a competition is in progress.

<Card title="Most Wanted" icon="bolt" href="/field-tech/most-wanted">
  Full guide: point tiers, the queue, the leaderboard, and sprint competitions.

## Sprint banner

When your admin has an active sprint running, an orange banner appears below the Quick Actions grid. It shows:

| Element | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Sprint name** | The name your admin gave this competition window |
| **Countdown** | How much time is left in the sprint |
| **My Score** | Your total points earned so far this sprint |
| **Rank** | Your current position on the sprint leaderboard |

Tap **View Queue** to open the Most Wanted queue and start earning points.

---

## Leaderboard preview

Below the sprint banner (or in its place when no sprint is active), a top-3 leaderboard shows the leading techs for the current month. Tap **See all** to open the full leaderboard in Most Wanted.

---

## Footer

The footer is always visible at the bottom of the screen:

**Help & Docs** — Opens the Forager documentation site in your browser.

**Contact Support** — Composes an email to the Hiverise Labs support team.

**App version** — The installed version number, useful when contacting support.
