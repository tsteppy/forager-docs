---
title: "Floor Map Rounds"
description: "How to use the floor map view to run visual confirmation rounds on a familiar floor"
---

The Floor Map view gives you a bird's-eye view of a floor with every room's attestation status overlaid as colored pins. It is the fastest way to do a confirmation round when you already know the layout — walk the floor, spot the red pins, tap to confirm.

## Opening the floor map

In the app, navigate to your location hierarchy (**Manage Locations** or the building → floor list). Each floor that has a floor plan uploaded shows a map icon to the right of its name. Tap the map icon to open the Floor Map view for that floor.

If no map icon appears, the floor plan has not been uploaded yet. Ask your admin to add one from the dashboard.

## Reading the pin colors

Each room with at least one tracked asset shows a pin on the floor plan. Pin color reflects how recently the room was last confirmed:

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Green | Confirmed within the last 7 days |
| Amber | Last confirmed 7–30 days ago |
| Red | Not confirmed in over 30 days, or never confirmed |

These thresholds match the staleness indicators on the web dashboard, so what you see in the app lines up with what your manager sees in the dashboard.

## Filtering by asset type

A row of filter chips runs along the top of the screen — one chip per asset type present on the floor (for example, **Printers**, **Ventilators**, **Workstations**). Tapping a chip shows only the pins for rooms that contain that asset type. Tap it again to deselect.

When one or more filters are active, a count appears showing how many rooms are currently visible out of the total on the floor (e.g., **"4 of 12 rooms shown"**). This is useful when you're responsible for a specific asset category and want to work through only those rooms.

## Confirming a room

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap a room pin">
    Tap any pin on the floor plan. A bottom sheet slides up showing the room name and asset type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Confirm Here">
    Tap **Confirm Here** to record that you have physically visited and verified the assets in that room. The pin immediately turns green with a checkmark.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move to the next pin">
    Tap **Cancel** to dismiss the sheet without confirming, or simply tap another pin to move on.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigating a dense floor plan

The floor plan supports pinch-to-zoom and two-finger pan so you can work precisely on floors with many rooms close together. Double-tap anywhere to zoom in on that area. Tap the reset button in the top-right corner at any time to return to the full-floor fit-to-screen view.

<Note>
  Floor Map Rounds confirm that you have been in the room — they do not require scanning individual asset barcodes. For full asset-level attestation, use the standard scanning flow in Survey Mode or the home screen scanner.
</Note>
