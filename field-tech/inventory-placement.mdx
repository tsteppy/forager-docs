## When to Use This

Use **Place Assets** when your team is running a wall-to-wall inventory and floor plan images are already uploaded but zones haven't been named yet.

You don't need to know room names. Tap where the asset is on the map — an admin will name the zones later.

## How to Place an Asset

1. From the home screen, tap **Place Assets**
2. Select a floor
3. Tap **Scan & Place** and scan the asset barcode (or use your hardware trigger)
4. The floor plan enters tap mode — tap where the asset is
5. A pin appears. Repeat for the next asset.

## Pin Colors

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Orange | Placed by you this session |
| Gray | Placed, but not yet assigned to a named zone |
| Green | Placed and assigned to a zone |

## What Happens If an Asset Isn't in the Roster?

If a scanned barcode isn't in the asset roster, Forager creates a minimal record for it automatically. An admin can fill in the name, type, and other details later.

## Leaving a Session

Tap the back button to see your session summary. Your placements are saved even if you exit the app.
