---
title: "Most Wanted"
description: "How to use the Most Wanted queue to find the highest-value assets to confirm"
---

Most Wanted is a prioritized list of assets that need presence confirmation. It surfaces the assets where a confirmed sighting is most overdue — the ones that earn the most points — so you can make the biggest impact in the least time.

Open it from the **⚡ Most Wanted** button on the home screen.

---

## The Queue tab

The Queue shows assets ranked by how long it has been since anyone confirmed their location. Each card shows:

| Field | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Asset name / tag** | The device identifier |
| **Last location** | The room where it was last confirmed |
| **Days since last seen** | How long ago a confirmation was recorded |
| **Points badge** | How many points confirming this asset earns |

### Point tiers

| Badge | Points | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| 🔥 | 10 pts | Never confirmed, or not confirmed in 90+ days |
| ⚡ | 3 pts | Not confirmed in 30–89 days |
| ✓ | 1 pt | Confirmed within the last 30 days |

The 🔥 assets are your highest-priority targets. These are the devices most at risk of being in the wrong location or missing entirely.

### Filtering

Use the filter chips at the top to narrow the queue:

| Filter | Shows |
|---|---|
| **All** | Every asset pending confirmation |
| **🔥 10 pts** | Only the highest-value never/long-overdue assets |
| **⚡ 3 pts** | Assets not confirmed in 30–89 days |

### Locked assets

A card showing 🔒 **Locked** has already been confirmed by another tech during the current sprint. It will not earn you points and cannot be re-confirmed until the sprint ends.

### Starting a confirmation

Tap any unlocked card to go directly to the Presence Confirmation screen. Scan the asset's barcode — you do not need to manually locate the asset first.

<Tip>
  Work through the 🔥 cards first. Ten-point assets take the same amount of time as one-point assets but contribute far more to your score.
</Tip>

## The Leaderboard tab

The Leaderboard shows how techs on your team rank by total confirmation points.

### Time periods

Use the filter chips to switch between views:

| Period | Shows |
|---|---|
| **Sprint** | Points earned during the current active sprint only (visible when a sprint is running) |
| **This Month** | Points earned in the current calendar month |
| **All Time** | Cumulative points across all time |

Your row is highlighted in blue. The top three ranked techs display medal icons.

---

## Points and sprints

### Earning points

Points are recorded the moment you confirm an asset. The number of points depends on how stale the asset was at the time of the scan — it is calculated automatically. You cannot earn points by scanning an asset that is already locked by another tech in the same sprint.

### Sprints

A sprint is a time-limited competition set up by your admin — typically a week or two. During a sprint:

- The **Sprint** leaderboard period appears on the Leaderboard tab
- A sprint banner appears on your home screen showing your current score and rank
- Assets confirmed during the sprint are locked and cannot be re-confirmed by other techs until the sprint ends

When a sprint ends, points roll into the **All Time** total and the next sprint (if scheduled) begins.

<Note>
  If no sprint is currently active, the Sprint filter on the Leaderboard tab is hidden. Points still accumulate toward This Month and All Time.
</Note>

## Points earned sheet

After each successful confirmation, a sheet pops up showing how many points you just earned:

| Badge | Label |
|---|---|
| 🔥 | 10 pts |
| ⚡ | 3 pts |
| ✓ | 1 pt |

**Done** — dismisses the sheet and returns you to scanning mode.

**Next in queue →** — dismisses the sheet and navigates back to the Most Wanted queue so you can pick the next target without interrupting your flow.
